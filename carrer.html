Career opportunities in food processing

Overall employment of agricultural and food scientists is projected to grow 6 percent from 2019 to 2029, faster than the average for all occupations. Employment of agricultural and food scientists is projected to grow as research into agricultural production methods and techniques continues.

1)Now, more than ever, people are mindful about the food they eat, its source, quality, nutrition, convenience, and cost. The most important of all “Is it safe for consumption?”. This is where Food technologists come in, food processing industry is very vast and versatile, and it provides promising career opportunities to the young generation.
